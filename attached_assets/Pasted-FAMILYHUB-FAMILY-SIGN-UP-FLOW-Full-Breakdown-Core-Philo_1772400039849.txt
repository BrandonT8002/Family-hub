FAMILYHUB — FAMILY SIGN-UP FLOW (Full Breakdown)

Core Philosophy

Sign-up should:

• Feel welcoming
• Feel structured
• Feel intentional
• Feel secure
• Not feel overwhelming
• Not feel corporate

The owner should fall in love with the app before inviting anyone.

We build emotional buy-in first.

⸻

PHASE 1 — Account Creation (Owner Only)

Step 1: Welcome Screen

Tone:

“Welcome to FamilyHub.”
“Let’s build your household.”

Two buttons:
• Get Started
• Log In

⸻

Step 2: Personal Information

Owner enters:

• First Name
• Last Name
• Date of Birth
• Email
• Password
• Confirm Password

Optional:
• Phone number (for 2FA)

Age verification:
• Must be 18+ to create a family account

Clear note:
“You’ll be the owner of this household.”

⸻

Step 3: Security Setup

• Enable biometric login (optional)
• Enable two-factor authentication (optional but encouraged)

This reinforces trust immediately.

⸻

PHASE 2 — Choose Family Tier

After account creation, owner selects family plan.

No free tier.
First week trial included.

⸻

Tier Options (Family Version Only)

Family Core

Up to 2 members
Best for couples or single parent + child

Family Plus

Up to 5 members
Best for small households

Family Extended

Up to 10 members
Best for larger families / blended families

Important:
Even if family has 3 members, they can choose Extended if desired.

We respect choice.

⸻

Tier Screen Messaging

“Choose the structure that fits your household.”

Show:

• Member capacity
• Caregiver slots
• Full access to features
• 7-day trial

Owner selects plan.
No immediate payment charge until trial ends.

⸻

PHASE 3 — Owner Profile Setup

Now we personalize.

Owner builds:

• Profile photo (optional)
• Theme preference
• Dashboard preference
• Notification style
• Tone preference (focused / calm / balanced)

We let them see the personality of the app.

⸻

PHASE 4 — Invite Members

After owner profile setup:

Screen says:

“Ready to build your household?”

Options:

• Invite Family Member
• Skip for now

Owner can invite via:

• Email
• Username
• Link

Important:

12 and under:
• No email required
• Parent creates username + password for child

13+:
• Email optional but encouraged
• At 13, prompt to add email with owner approval

⸻

PHASE 5 — Permission Setup (Light Version)

For each member added:

Owner chooses:

• Can create shared events?
• Can delete items?
• Can view finances?
• Can share media?
• Can message outside family?
• Can create private goals?

Not overwhelming.
Just essential toggles.

Advanced permissions can be adjusted later in Settings.

⸻

PHASE 6 — Home Dashboard Setup

Before landing on main screen:

We ask:

“What would you like to see first when you open the app?”

Options:

• Today’s Schedule
• Personal Dashboard (news, sports, quotes)
• Goals
• Money Overview
• Family Chat

User selects primary landing tab.

Can change anytime.

⸻

PHASE 7 — Quick Guided Tutorial (Short & Smooth)

5–6 screens max.

Not overwhelming.

⸻

Slide 1 — Schedule

“Organize personal and shared events.”

Slide 2 — Goals

“Track what matters most.”

Slide 3 — Money

“Understand where your money goes.”

Slide 4 — Shopping

“Stay organized in every store.”

Slide 5 — Privacy

“Private by default. Shared by choice.”

Slide 6 — You’re Ready

“Let’s build something strong.”

Button:
Enter FamilyHub

⸻

After Tutorial — Landing Screen

Owner lands on:

Their personalized dashboard.

Top section:
Encouraging message.

Example:
“Let’s set the tone for today.”

If no schedule yet:
Prompt:
“Add your first event.”

If no goals yet:
Prompt:
“Start your first goal.”

Gentle direction.
No pressure.

⸻

Important Structural Rules

• Owner must fall in love before inviting others
• Invitations optional — can build alone first
• Personalization happens before social
• Permissions structured but not overwhelming
• 12 and under simplified onboarding
• 13 prompts email upgrade
• 15–17 gradually increased autonomy

⸻

Emotional Intent of Sign-Up

The user should feel:

• In control
• Respected
• Organized
• Calm
• Inspired

Not:

• Tracked
• Watched
• Overwhelmed
• Rushed

⸻

Final Flow Summary
	1.	Welcome
	2.	Personal info
	3.	Security setup
	4.	Choose family tier
	5.	Owner personalization
	6.	Invite members
	7.	Permission light setup
	8.	Choose dashboard style
	9.	Quick tutorial
	10.	Enter app
