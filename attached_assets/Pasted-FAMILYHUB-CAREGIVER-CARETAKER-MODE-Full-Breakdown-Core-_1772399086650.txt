FAMILYHUB — CAREGIVER / CARETAKER MODE (Full Breakdown)

Core Purpose

Caregiver Mode is designed for:

• Babysitters
• Nannies
• Grandparents
• Trusted relatives
• Special needs aides
• Temporary guardians

It allows families to include trusted help without giving them full family access.

It is structured access, not family membership.

⸻

1. Caregiver Is NOT a Full Family Member

Caregivers:

• Do not count as regular adult family members
• Do not get full family visibility
• Do not access finances
• Do not access diary
• Do not access private chats
• Cannot change permissions
• Cannot add members

They are limited-access users.

⸻

2. How Caregiver Is Added

Owner adds caregiver through:

Settings → Add Caregiver

Owner sets:

• Access duration (temporary or ongoing)
• What child/individual they are responsible for
• What features they can access
• Chat permissions
• Schedule permissions

Caregiver must accept invitation.

⸻

3. Caregiver Access Options

Owner chooses what caregiver can see:

A) Schedule Access

• See assigned events only
OR
• See child’s schedule only
OR
• See shared family events

Cannot see:
• Private adult schedules
• Private teen schedules

⸻

B) Care Notes Access

Caregiver can log:

• Feeding times
• Diaper changes
• Medication given
• Nap times
• Behavioral notes
• Mood notes

This integrates with:

Baby / Special Needs Care Logs.

Parents always see logs.
Caregiver cannot delete past entries unless allowed.

⸻

C) Limited Chat Access

Caregiver can:

• Message assigned parents
• Message about assigned child
• Participate in dedicated caregiver group chat

Caregiver cannot:

• Access full family chat
• See private adult chats
• See teen private chats

⸻

D) Media Access

Caregiver can:

• Upload photos related to child
• Share updates

Owner controls:

• Whether caregiver can download media
• Whether caregiver can share media outside app

Minors’ media sharing controlled by owner.

⸻

4. Duration Control

Caregiver access can be:

• One-day access
• Weekend access
• Weekly recurring
• Ongoing

Owner can revoke at any time.

If revoked:
• Immediate removal
• No residual access
• Data remains in family logs

⸻

5. Capacity Limits

Caregiver accounts are limited.

Purpose:

• Prevent free expansion loophole
• Prevent business abuse
• Protect family structure

Exact limits can vary by subscription tier.

⸻

6. Caregiver Dashboard

Caregiver sees simplified dashboard:

• Assigned child
• Today’s schedule
• Care log entry button
• Contact parent button

No financial widgets.
No goals tab.
No diary tab.
No money tab.

Clean and focused.

⸻

7. Emergency Controls

Parents can enable:

• Emergency contact access
• Quick call button
• Medical info (optional)
• Allergy list

Caregiver sees only what parent allows.

⸻

8. Privacy Protection

Caregiver cannot:

• View financial data
• View wishlist (unless shared)
• View goals (unless shared)
• View adult chat
• View private schedule
• See diary

Caregiver does not become part of permanent family structure.

⸻

9. Care Notes System (Integrated)

Care Notes allow logging:

• Time-stamped entries
• Editable by parent
• Locked after 24 hours (optional setting)

Care Notes help:

• Shift change handoffs
• Consistency tracking
• Special needs documentation
• Medication tracking

Parents can download/export care logs if needed (future phase).

⸻

10. Abuse Reporting & Safety

Caregiver can:

• Report concern to owner
• Flag safety issue
• Log incident report

Parents can:

• Remove caregiver immediately
• Lock caregiver account

⸻

11. Cross-Family Considerations

Caregiver does not gain access to:

• Connected outside families
• Shared external wishlists
• External chats

Access is strictly internal.

⸻

12. Emotional Impact

This feature:

• Reduces parent anxiety
• Increases accountability
• Supports blended families
• Supports special needs households
• Makes shift transitions smoother

If removed:
Parents would feel less supported and less organized.

⸻

13. MVP Version

For beta:

• Add caregiver
• Assign child
• Limited schedule access
• Care log entry
• Parent messaging
• Revoke access

Advanced layers (medical, exports, etc.) can come later.

⸻

14. Business Protection

Caregiver mode cannot:

• Replace business version
• Scale into workforce management
• Circumvent subscription limits

It remains family-support-focused.
